## Conclusion

- **0 illegal moves and 0 stuck games** across 6,000 mixed-difficulty games, 5,000
  symmetric-difficulty games, and 600 cross-mode spot-check games (11,600 total) — the
  smoke suite's guarantees hold at full scale.
- **Symmetric-difficulty win rates are sane**: every difficulty sits within ~2.4 points of
  50/50 (478–524 wins per 1,000 for either team) when both teams play the same strength —
  no systematic seat/turn-order bias. Expert and Master are identical as expected (Master
  reuses Expert's strategy per CLAUDE.md).
- **Skill actually wins**: all three placeholder acceptance criteria pass — Hard beats
  Easy 60.5% (≥55% required), Hard beats Medium 56.1% (≥55% required), Expert beats Easy
  60.5% (≥60% required, passes with a ~0.5-point margin). Expert vs Hard and Master vs
  Expert sit close to 52%, which is expected — both AIs are using comparably strong
  heuristics, so the gap should be small.
- **Average round length is stable** (59–68 turns) and well within `maxTurns: 1000`.
- No balance changes are recommended from this run. The Expert-vs-Easy margin (60.5%
  against a 60% floor) is the thinnest of the three criteria and worth re-checking if the
  Expert heuristic weights change in the future.
